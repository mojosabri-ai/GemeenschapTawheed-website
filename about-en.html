About Us

The face behind the initiative for transparency

Introduction: Why I Share This With You

Assalamu Alaikum,

In a process where we advocate for greater openness and transparency within our mosque, I find it only logical that I myself am completely transparent with you. Trust is the foundation of our community, and that trust begins with knowing who the person is supporting this initiative.

I share my background with you so that you know that I speak not only as a committed brother, but also from a professional basis focused on honesty, proper administration, and compliance. My only goal is to use my experience and knowledge for the general benefit of our mosque, so that we can build together a future based on clear agreements and mutual respect.

Professional Background: Expertise & Integrity

Beyond my personal involvement with the mosque, I bring years of professional experience to guide this transparency process in an expert and ethical manner. My career is characterized by:

🎯

Strategic Leadership & Management

I have over 15 years of experience driving revenue and market share for international organizations. As Sales Director, I led teams of 31 FTE and was responsible for achieving complex strategic objectives.

⚙️

Business Operations & Process Optimization

I am an advocate of business transformation through Lean methodologies and am certified in Six Sigma (Green Belt). This enables me to improve processes and ensure organizational efficiency.

💬

Conflict Management & Communication

In addition to my business experience, I am a certified NLP Practitioner with expertise in Conflict Management and negotiation. I am a multilingual communicator, fluent in Dutch, English, and Arabic.

🎓

Education

I hold a Bachelor Degree in Business Management and IT and have additional qualifications in Human Potential Coaching.

🎯 My Goal

My goal is to use this business experience to help our association Tawheed transform into a transparent organization that fully complies with legal requirements and statutes.

💙 Our Shared Vision

I believe that by sharing this process with you, we not only strengthen our mosque, but also set an example of how a religious community can grow in a professional and transparent manner. This is good for our future and for all generations to come.

Building a Better Tawheed Together

I invite you to participate in this transformation process. Ask questions, provide feedback, and work together towards a mosque we can rightfully be proud of.
